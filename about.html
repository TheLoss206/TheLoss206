About The Loss

The Loss formed in Seattle, Washington in 2010, carving out a sound rooted in melodic punk with an edge shaped by urgency, honesty, and community. Active from 2010 to 2016, the band built a home and following through relentless shows, DIY ethics, and a commitment to writing songs that meant something to the people in the room.

During their original run, The Loss released two records on San Diego’s La Escalera Records — Everything You Ever Wanted (2012) and Last Rites (2016). Along the way, they were fortunate to share stages with friends and peers including Burn Burn Burn, Random Orbits, Success, Poke Da Squid, and many more.

In 2023 and 2024, The Loss reunited for a series of shows at the place they called home — The Kraken Bar and Lounge — celebrating the music, the friendships, and the years that shaped the band.

RIP The Loss (2024). VIVA LA LEVA.
